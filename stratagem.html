Stratagem

Integrate urban to rural (team size 1-3)

It’s relatively easier to cater the riches with extraordinary products bearing the ‘beyond ordinary’ price tags. The challenge is to produce the exact same product with assured quality and enhanced affordability for the rural markets. The competition is designed specially to integrate the developed urban society with our roots: the rural markets.

The participants must first obtain their products by deciphering the instructions given in the first round.

The next round challenges their financial management where they develop strategies to cut the costs and ensure profitability. This is where they try to retain the quality while narrow down the pricing. The grand finale would include allocating the budget to various resources and creating a marketing strategy.
